Holi

Holi is a spring festival also known as festival of colors and sometimes festival of love. It is an ancient Hindu religious festival which has become popular with non-Hindus in many parts of South Asia as well as people of other communities. Holi celebrations start with a Holika bonfire on the night before Holl where people gather, sing and dance.
